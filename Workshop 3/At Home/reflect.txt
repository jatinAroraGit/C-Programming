The conditional selection construct is shorthand for the alternative path construct.
This ternary expression combines a condition and two sub-expressions using the ? : operator.

SYNTAX-
 condition ? operand : operand
If the condition is true, the expression evaluates to the operand between ? and :.
  If the condition is false, the expression evaluates to the operand following :.
